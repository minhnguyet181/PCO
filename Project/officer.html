OUR OFFICER

Welcome to the Office Locations page of our prestigious luxury women's clothing brand. We are committed to providing exceptional fashion experiences to our discerning clientele, and our dedication extends to our offices around the world. Our brand represents timeless elegance, unparalleled quality, and impeccable style.

Explore the following office locations where our creative and passionate teams work tirelessly to bring our vision to life:

Headquarters - New York City, USA:

Our global headquarters resides in the vibrant and fashion-forward city of New York. Located in the heart of Manhattan, our headquarters houses various departments, including design, marketing, sales, and operations. It serves as the creative hub where innovative ideas are born and our collections are meticulously crafted.

Design Studio - Milan, Italy

Nestled in the fashion capital of the world, our design studio in Milan is where our talented team of designers turns dreams into reality. Inspired by the rich history of Italian fashion, this office is a melting pot of creativity, where sketches come to life and exquisite garments take shape.
